With more than 30 waterfalls above the height of 10 m, Sri Lanka is a treasure trove for all these mesmerising cascades that it keeps safe in its nooks and corners. Here's our list of some of the best waterfalls in Sri Lanka:

An hour away from Ella, Diyaluma Falls is the second-highest among the waterfalls in Sri Lanka. The waterfall tumbles down from a height of a whopping 220 meters, with several beautiful natural infinity pools at different levels along its length. Diyaluma Falls forms along the Poonagala Oya, a minor tributary of the river Kirindi Oya. The highlight of Diyaluma Falls is the hike to its top, swimming in the serene pools, and the magnificent views of the emerald hills.
